Hi there! I'm Sarah, a first year PhD student in the Geomicrobiolology group at The University of Manchester. My research is about how bacteria can affect the disposal of radioactive waste. Seem extreme? That's because it is! The bacteria that I work with are extremophiles, meaning that they can live happily in extreme environments. This can be both beneficial and problematic when it comes to how we dispose of our radioactive waste. For example, they may be able to help reduce mobility of radionuclides such as uranium, which is great!
